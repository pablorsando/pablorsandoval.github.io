Materialistic Nate Project

Materialistic Nate project involves photo taking between Mother nature's material and human interaction. After I got the ideal clicked in my mind, I began to see and visualize places, objects, and people that others normally won't bother to take the picture. Whether it's nature's material's material or human involvement I found ways to overcome and represent the topic of this project.
